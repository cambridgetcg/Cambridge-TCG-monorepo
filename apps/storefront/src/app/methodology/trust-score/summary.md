# Trust score — TLDR

A 0–100 number summarising your track record on Cambridge TCG. Computed from completion rate, review score (reviewer-weighted), trade volume (logarithmic), account age, and external reputation; penalised by open disputes, lost disputes, recent refunds, and active flags. Drives trade limits, escrow tier, and payout hold.
