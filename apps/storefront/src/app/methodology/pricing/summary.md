# Pricing — TLDR

How Cambridge TCG computes each card's **policy-bound reference price** — never an offer or open-data grant (the platform stopped selling and buying on 2026-07-06). JPY → GBP at a captured FX rate, plus margin, VAT, and rounding. Shop-era channels are documented historically so old numbers stay reproducible; executable prices are collectors' own bids and asks on the market.
