# Pricing — TLDR

How Cambridge TCG computes each card's **publicly viewable reference price** — never an offer (the platform stopped selling and buying on 2026-07-06). Reuse follows the source rights declared by the API response. JPY → GBP at a captured FX rate, plus margin, VAT, and rounding. Shop-era channels are documented historically; executable prices are collectors' own bids and asks.
