# Pricing — TLDR

How the kingdom computes each card's **reference price** — a policy-bound derived guide, never an offer or open-data grant (the platform stopped selling and buying on 2026-07-06). JPY → GBP at a captured FX rate, plus margin, VAT, and rounding. The shop-era channels (Shopify, eBay, trade-in cash/credit) are documented historically so old numbers stay reproducible; the prices you can trade at are collectors' own bids and asks on the market.
