# Pricing — TLDR

How the kingdom computes each card's **reference price** — a Cambridge-authored CC0 methodology, never an offer (the platform stopped selling and buying on 2026-07-06). Published price observations are separate records: they default to `NOASSERTION`, carry their source rights, and are not made reusable merely by public access. JPY → GBP at a captured FX rate, plus margin, VAT, and rounding. The shop-era channels (Shopify, eBay, trade-in cash/credit) are documented historically so old numbers stay reproducible; the prices you can trade at are collectors' own bids and asks on the market.
