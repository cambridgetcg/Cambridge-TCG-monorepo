# UK collector events commons — TLDR

A four-event, source-backed demonstrator of public UK trading-card events, venues, and organisations. Every trust-bearing field keeps evidence; conflicts remain visible; unknown accessibility stays unknown; personal people and direct personal contacts are excluded. Mixed upstream facts are `NOASSERTION`; Cambridge-authored schemas and methodology are CC0. The broader UK Card Shows listing set remains link-only unless permission or an open-data grant supports expansion.

Builder formats: [JSON](/api/v1/collector-events), [iCalendar](/api/v1/collector-events/calendar.ics), [GeoJSON](/api/v1/collector-events/map.geojson), and [JSON Schema](/api/v1/collector-events/schema). Coverage is explicitly incomplete at [/api/v1/collector-events/coverage](/api/v1/collector-events/coverage).
