# Store credit — TLDR (historical)

Store credit was the shop-era non-money currency: earned via cashback, trade-in quotes, and rewards; spent as a one-shot Stripe coupon at the shop's checkout. The shop era ended 2026-07-06 with **zero balances outstanding** (0 users, £0.00) — nothing stranded. The `store_credit_ledger` table stays; history is history. Selling now happens between collectors on the market, in cash.
