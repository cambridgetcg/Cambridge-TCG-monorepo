# Universal representation — TLDR

Every card on the platform has a sibling endpoint that returns its data in a language-free, substrate-free encoding: cryptographic hashes, ratios, ISO-epoch timestamps, typed-graph edges. Decoderable by any computing intelligence regardless of evolutionary history — and honest about what *can't* translate (aesthetic meaning, cultural connotation).
