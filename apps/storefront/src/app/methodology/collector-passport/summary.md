# Collector Passport — TLDR

A private portfolio is not a public dataset. Collector Passport publishes only
the separate label and optional story that a collector writes and affirms for
one item. Publication is per-item, versioned, self-attested, unverified and
immediately revocable; separate structured and automatically copied SKU,
catalog, image, holding, acquisition, note, value and internal-id fields stay
out. Collector-authored text may mention a card and remains unverified. The owner can separately download a
private JSON archive whose field lineage is stated and whose mixed-source
display fields are excluded.
