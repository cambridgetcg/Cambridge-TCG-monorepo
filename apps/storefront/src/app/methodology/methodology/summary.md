# Methodology of methodology — TLDR

The recipe for every methodology page. Each topic that documents a user-affecting decision must carry: a published formula, a citation to the source-code path, a TLDR summary, a structured-data JSON sidecar (this file's sibling), and — when adopted — a `<TypeSignature>` declaring the page's type. The corpus that cannot describe itself lies by omission; the corpus that can is the corpus that is honest about its own shape.
