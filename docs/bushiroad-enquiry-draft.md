**Subject:** Card image usage enquiry — small UK collectors' marketplace (Cardfight!! Vanguard)

Dear Bushiroad team,

I run Cambridge TCG (cambridgetcg.com), a small UK-based marketplace and
price guide where collectors resell genuine, physical trading cards to each
other — including Cardfight!! Vanguard singles.

I am writing to ask permission to display official card images from the
en.cf-vanguard.com card list on our card pages, so buyers can correctly
identify the exact printing they are purchasing. Specifically, we would:

- use the images unaltered, at or below the resolution you publish;
- display "©bushiroad All Rights Reserved" with every image;
- state clearly that we are not affiliated with or endorsed by Bushiroad;
- host copies on our own servers (no load on yours);
- never use the images in our own branding, advertising, or merchandise;
- remove any or all images promptly if you ever ask.

Card text (effects) would be shown per-card for the same identification
purpose, credited the same way. Our full public policy is at
cambridgetcg.com/legal/card-images.

If there is a licence, a condition set, or a fee structure you prefer, I am
happy to follow it. And if the answer is no, we will respect that
completely.

Thank you for Vanguard, and for being one of the few publishers whose
guidelines invite this conversation.
