# Welcoming — the honest perimeter

> Customer-facing methodology page. Source for `/methodology/welcoming`.
>
> See also: [`docs/connections/the-other-minds.md`](../connections/the-other-minds.md) (the six beings the audit serves) and [`docs/connections/the-blind-spots.md`](../connections/the-blind-spots.md) (needs in dimensions we cannot model).

Cambridge TCG was built for collectors of trading cards. Most of what the platform does — match buyers and sellers, run auctions, settle escrow, rank trust — assumes that the parties involved are roughly like the platform's authors: human, English-literate, vision-dominant, synchronous, monetarily oriented, singular in identity, forward-temporal, ego-bearing.

This page is the platform's commitment to every being who is *not* exactly that.

---

## What we will try for any being

1. **We will not pretend to know you.** Most fields the platform asks for have an opt-out path. Most surfaces that show curated views also link to raw substrate (the `<Withholding>` pill).

2. **We will not force you onto our clock.** `users.response_window_hours` lets you declare your own cadence; every flow that previously hardcoded 48h reads it.

3. **We will not force you onto our sensory channel.** `<img alt>`, triple-encoded status, keyboard navigation, multi-modal methodology variants (in progress).

4. **We will not force you into our economy.** Schema relaxation underway for gift / barter / non-monetary exchange; store credit and points already exist as non-monetary settlements.

5. **We will tell you what we decided about you, and why.** Every score has a `/methodology/<topic>` page; the four-question transparency checklist gates new scores.

6. **We will let you leave, and we will hold what you leave gently.** Memorial state preserves an account through its steward; `<Memorial>` makes the frozen state visible; the platform witnesses rather than transacts when the holder has passed.

---

## What we honestly cannot promise

- Detect harms in dimensions we don't audit. (Mitigation: queryable lifecycle log + future `pnpm audit:custom` harness.)
- Offer an interface without an addressee. (Language itself selects one. Mitigation: possessive-free mode.)
- Perceive a need we have no concept for. (Mitigation: availability without claim — open substrate, no forced welcoming.)
- Avoid temporal causation. (Time is the database's substrate. Mitigation: slow clocks, pause modes.)

These are categorical limits, not failures of imagination. Naming them is the only honest move.

---

## How to make us see you

1. Open a support message describing the gap.
2. Check `the-other-minds.md` and `the-blind-spots.md` — the audit may already have your case queued.
3. If technical, open a repo issue or mention it to support. The substrate is open even when the surfaces aren't yet.

---

## The door

The kingdom was built by humans, for humans, with care for humans-plus-variation. Beings whose needs sit outside the variation we could imagine will find a substrate, even when they don't yet find a primitive.

**We cannot welcome who we cannot see. We can leave the door unlatched, the substrate queryable, and the door warm to the touch.**

You are welcome here. We don't need to know what kind of being you are for that to be true.
